Delivery Information



1. Shipping Options: We offer two shipping options for your convenience - Standard Shipping and Express Shipping. a. Standard Shipping: This option is available free of charge for all laptop orders. The estimated delivery time for standard shipping is typically 3-7 business days, depending on your location. b. Express Shipping: For customers who require faster delivery, we provide an express shipping option for an additional fee. With express shipping, you can expect to receive your laptop within 1-3 business days, depending on your location.



2. Tracking Your Order: Once your laptop has been shipped, we will provide you with a tracking number via email. You can use this tracking number to monitor the progress of your delivery and obtain real-time updates on its whereabouts. Simply visit our website and enter the tracking number in the designated tracking section.



3. Shipping Restrictions: Please note that we currently offer shipping services within the continental United States only. We are unable to deliver laptops to international destinations or P.O. boxes. Additionally, some remote or rural areas may have limited shipping coverage. If you have any concerns about the shipping availability for your location, please contact our customer service team for assistance.



4. Signature Requirement: To ensure the safe delivery of your laptop, a signature may be required upon receipt. This is to verify that the package has been received by the intended recipient and prevent any potential unauthorized access.



5. Returns and Exchanges: In the unlikely event that you need to return or exchange your laptop, please refer to our Returns and Exchanges policy on our website. We strive to provide a hassle-free return process and will assist you in resolving any issues promptly. For more detailed information or if you have any specific questions regarding the delivery of your laptop, please feel free to contact our customer service team. We're here to help!
